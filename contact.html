Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical fencing project take?

Project duration depends on the size and complexity. A typical residential fencing project takes 3-7 days, while larger commercial projects may take 2-4 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during consultation.

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes, we provide completely free estimates and consultations. Our team will visit your site, assess your requirements, and provide a detailed quote with no obligation.

What warranty do you offer on your work?

We offer a 2-year warranty on workmanship for all our projects. Material warranties vary by manufacturer, and we'll help you claim them if needed.

Do you work in areas outside Kadi?

Yes, we serve the entire Gujarat region. While we're based in Kadi, we regularly take projects in nearby cities and districts. Contact us to discuss your location.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, bank transfers, and digital payments. Payment terms are typically 50% advance, 40% on completion, and 10% final payment after satisfaction.
